## Overview

**Moments** is a media editing and optimization platform that enables users to edit **images and videos directly in the browser**.  
The app is powered by **ImageKit**, leveraging its real-time transformation and CDN delivery capabilities.

Users can upload raw media and instantly apply transformations such as cropping, resizing, format conversion, and optimization â€” making it ideal for **social media, websites, and online stores**.


## Key Features

### **Features implemented**

- **Image Upload & Management**: Upload and organize images
- **Image Cropping & Resizing**: Dynamic crop, scale, and aspect ratio control
- **Format Conversion**: Auto-convert images (JPEG, PNG, WebP, AVIF)
- **Image Optimization**: Automatic quality and size optimization
- **Video Upload & Processing**: Upload and manage videos
- **Video Transformations**: Resize, trim, and optimize videos
- **Real-time Preview**: Instantly preview transformations
- **CDN Delivery**: Fast global delivery via ImageKit CDN
