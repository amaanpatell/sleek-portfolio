## Overview

**ProjectCamp** is a project management application designed for individuals and small teams to plan, track, and collaborate on work efficiently.  
Users can create projects, break them into tasks, assign work to different team members, and maintain structured notes for better clarity.

The platform focuses on **clean workflows, structured data, and team collaboration**.

## Key Features

### **Features implemented**

- **Project Management**: Create and manage multiple projects
- **Task Management**: Add, update, and delete tasks within projects
- **Task Assignment**: Assign tasks to different users
- **Task Status Tracking**: Track progress (To-Do, In Progress, Completed)
- **Notes & Sub-notes**: Add detailed notes and nested sub-notes
- **User Collaboration**: Invite and manage project members
- **Access Control**: Permissions based on user roles
